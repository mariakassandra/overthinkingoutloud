Hello, I'm the Overthinker

Welcome to my corner of the internet where I turn my constant stream of thoughts into something resembling art. I'm an expert at analyzing social situations long after they're over, finding meaning in the most mundane encounters, and staying awake contemplating whether I used the right tone in an email from 2017.

When I'm not overthinking, I'm probably reading books, writing poetry, or documenting my dating misadventures. This blog is where all those thoughts that usually just bounce around my head find a home.
